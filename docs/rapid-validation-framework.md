# BYI Rapid Validation Framework — Pick Your Offer

**Owner:** Barry Young — The Blueprint Builder

**Goal:** Validate market demand for your favorite BYI offer in **≤ 14 days**
using small, decisive experiments.

## 0) Quick Pick

Which offer are we testing first?

1. Blueprint-to-Business Accelerator
2. Legacy Business Blueprint
3. Solopreneur Systems Design
4. Purpose-Driven Brand Story Mapping

Pick the offer above. Everything below is pre-scaffolded for all four: run the
**Core Sequence**, then apply the **Offer-Specific Add-Ons**.

## 1) Core BYI Validation Sequence (7 Steps)

**BYI Terms:** Foundation → Friction → Blueprint → Build → Signal → Proof → Next Build

1. **Foundation (Hypothesis & Success Gate)**
   - Hypothesis template: *"For [ICP], a [offer] that delivers [primary outcome]
     will drive [metric] within 14 days at a test price of $[X]."*
   - Success gates (pick 2+): ≥ 200 unique visitors; ≥ 25 qualified leads; ≥ 5
     pre-orders/deposits; ≥ 8 interviews booked; ≥ $[X] collected.
2. **Friction (Problem Inventory)**
   - List the top 5 pains your ICP says in their own words (from DMs/forums).
     Rank by **Cost of Delay** and **Frequency**.
3. **Blueprint (Offer Slice)**
   - Define a **Minimum Credible Transformation (MCT)** = the smallest outcome
     that still feels meaningful (e.g., "90-minute Clarity Blueprint + 7-day
     action plan").
4. **Build (Landing + Traffic)**
   - Launch a one-page BYI Landing (see Section 2) + 2 traffic taps (Section 3).
5. **Signal (Lead + Pre-Order)**
   - Capture email + a single qualifying question. Offer a **$49–$199 refundable
     deposit** or a **waitlist with date**.
6. **Proof (Interviews + Deposits)**
   - Run 8–12 short interviews (Section 4). Convert best-fit to deposit or pilot.
7. **Next Build (Decision)**
   - If you hit 2+ success gates → schedule pilot cohort/start dates. If not →
     adjust MCT, price, or ICP and rerun.

## 2) Landing Page Components to Test (A/B menu)

**Structure:** Above-the-fold clarity → Credibility → Outcome → How it works →
Proof → Offer → Objections → CTA

- **Headline (A/B):**
  - A: "Turn scattered ideas into a buildable business blueprint in 21 days."
  - B: "Architect your next 90 days: a step-by-step plan you'll actually follow."
- **Subhead (A/B):**
  - A: Outcome-first ("Leave with a visual plan, 3 key systems, and your first
    milestone.")
  - B: Pain-first ("Stop spinning. Start building. One blueprint, one clear
    path.")
- **Hero Visual:** Blueprint-style schematic of the transformation (before →
  after).
- **Credibility Strip:** "BYI Framework • 1:1 guidance • Implementation kit"
  (swap badges/testimonials as you collect them).
- **Offer Slice:** Name the MCT, deliverables, time to value, start date.
- **How It Works:** 3 steps (Map → Design → Build).
- **Risk Reversal (A/B):**
  - A: "Love-it or deposit refunded."
  - B: "If no plan by Day 7, full refund."
- **Objections Mini-FAQ:** Time, price, 'tried before,' accountability.
- **CTA (A/B):**
  - A: "Reserve your spot ($99 refundable)"
  - B: "Get my blueprint (apply in 60 sec)"
- **Price Anchor (A/B):** Show full value (e.g., $2,500) vs pilot price (e.g.,
  $499) — or show no price, application first.

**Copy blocks you can reuse:**

- *Proof of Value:* "Leave with a buildable plan, not a notebook of ideas."
- *Differentiator:* "Architect-style diagrams that make decisions obvious."

## 3) Traffic Taps & Communities (pick 2 to start)

**Low-lift channels:** Personal LinkedIn post + 10 warm DMs; 2 niche forums; 1
partner newsletter shoutout.

**Offer-Specific Communities:**

1. **Accelerator – New Solopreneurs:** Indie Hackers, r/Entrepreneur,
   r/smallbusiness, relevant Facebook groups (Solopreneur/Creator groups), local
   Chamber/SCORE, LinkedIn hashtag #buildinpublic.
2. **Legacy – Owners 40–60:** Local Chamber & Rotary, EO/Vistage-style networks,
   family-business & succession LinkedIn groups, CPA/estate-planning partners for
   intros.
3. **Systems Design – Coaches/Consultants:** Notion/Airtable/Zapier communities,
   r/Notion, r/automation, automation Slack groups, creators' newsletters.
4. **Brand Story Mapping – Thought Leaders:** r/copywriting, r/marketing,
   writer/operator communities (Write of Passage/Ship 30 alumni), podcast
   guesting groups, speaker bureaus.

**Outreach script (short):**

> "Building a small pilot to [outcome]. If this pain is live for you, I'll trade
> a free Blueprint Audit for 12 minutes of feedback. DM 'BLUEPRINT' and I'll send
> the one-pager."

## 4) Interview Guide (uncover real pain fast)

Keep to 12–15 minutes. Record permission first.

1. **Context:** "What are you building & where are you stuck this week?"
2. **Cost of Delay:** "If nothing changes in 90 days, what breaks?"
3. **Attempts:** "What have you already tried? What helped/hurt?"
4. **Decision Triggers:** "What must be true to say yes to help?"
5. **Constraints:** "Time & budget guardrails?"
6. **Success Picture:** "Imagine 30 days from now — what would make you say 'this
   changed the trajectory'?"
7. **Willingness to Act:** "If I gave you a 2-week blueprint with checkpoints,
   would you put down a small deposit to hold a start date?"
8. **Referrals:** "Who else should I speak with about this exact problem?"

*Note: Mirror exact phrasing they use; paste verbatims into your Problem
Inventory.*

## 5) Metrics that Matter (14-day targets)

- **Traffic:** 200–400 unique visitors.
- **Lead capture rate:** 10–25% (warm traffic closer to 25%).
- **Interview opt-in:** ≥ 8 booked (≥ 30% of leads scheduling).
- **Pre-order/deposit:** 2–5% of visitors or 20–40% of qualified calls.
- **Time to first signal:** ≤ 72 hours to first deposit or paid audit.
- **Price learning:** 3 price points tested or 1 anchor vs pilot price.

*Secondary:* CTA click-through, scroll depth, source→lead quality,
refund/withdrawal reasons.

## 6) Offer-Specific Add-Ons

### (1) Blueprint-to-Business Accelerator

- **Test angles:** "Clarity in 21 days" vs "First 3 paying clients in 45 days."
- **Lead magnet:** "90-Minute Micro-Blueprint Workshop" (limited seats).
- **Credibility:** Before/after roadmaps; checklists; day-by-day schedule.

### (2) Legacy Business Blueprint

- **Test angles:** "Succession without chaos" vs "Make the business
  transferable."
- **Lead magnet:** "Legacy Readiness Scorecard" (10 questions).
- **Credibility:** Confidentiality note; optional NDA; testimonials from advisors.

### (3) Solopreneur Systems Design

- **Test angles:** "Client onboarding in 48 hours" vs "Ship weekly with zero
  chaos."
- **Lead magnet:** "Plug-and-Play Notion/Airtable Pack."
- **Credibility:** GIF of system flow; 7-day implementation guarantee.

### (4) Purpose-Driven Brand Story Mapping

- **Test angles:** "Explain your value in one page" vs "Story that sells your next
  offer."
- **Lead magnet:** "Story Blocks: BYI Narrative Canvas."
- **Credibility:** Before/after homepage copy; pitch slide snippet.

## 7) Biggest Challenges by Audience

- **New Solopreneurs:** Idea overwhelm, fear of picking wrong niche, scattered
  execution, limited funds, course fatigue.
- **Owners 40–60 (Legacy):** Time scarcity, privacy concerns, emotional
  attachment, team buy-in, unclear successor.
- **Coaches/Consultants (Systems):** Tool overload, messy data, inconsistent
  delivery, adoption resistance, feast-or-famine cycles.
- **Thought Leaders (Story):** Message dilution, generic positioning,
  inconsistent content, website paralysis, weak CTA.

## 8) 14-Day BYI Sprint Plan

- **Day 1–2:** Foundation (hypothesis, MCT), draft landing, outreach list of 30.
- **Day 3–4:** Publish landing, post to 2 communities, 10 DMs/day.
- **Day 5–7:** 6–8 interviews, push deposits for pilot, iterate copy.
- **Day 8–10:** Second traffic tap, price/CTA test, add testimonial/proof.
- **Day 11–14:** Final push, tally metrics, go/no-go with next-build plan.

## 9) Follow-Up Questions (to sharpen the ICP)

1. Which offer are we testing? (1–4 above)
2. Who is the exact buyer? (role, revenue/stage, industry, geography)
3. What's their "load-bearing problem" this month?
4. What's a believable Minimum Credible Transformation for 14 days?
5. Pilot pricing comfort zone? (deposit $49–$199, pilot $299–$999, full
   $2K–$10K+)
6. Preferred delivery mode? (1:1, cohort, async + check-ins)
7. What objection worries you most to encounter?
8. What constraints do you want? (capacity, schedule, channels you enjoy)

## 10) Decision Gate & Next Build

- **Green:** Hit 2+ success gates → lock pilot start date; draft delivery
  schedule; announce cohort cap.
- **Yellow:** Hit 1 gate → tighten ICP and MCT; rerun 7 days.
- **Red:** Hit 0 gates → pivot angle, price, or offer slice; test a new traffic
  tap.
